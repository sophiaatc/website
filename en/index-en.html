Non-invasive Technology.

Our encapsulated execution architecture allows SOPHIA to operate on certified consoles (STVD) without altering original source code or compromising the security of the primary radar system.

netCDF4 processing

Direct insertion and processing of raw GOES 19 satellite data through an isolated dedicated server, ensuring high fidelity and low latency.
