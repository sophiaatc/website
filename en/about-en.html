About the company

Engineering oriented toward operational safety in ATS environments.

SOPHIA ATC develops solutions to expand situational awareness, reduce information fragmentation, and strengthen operational traceability in critical air navigation contexts.

Our Mission

To develop technological solutions that integrate multiple information layers in Air Traffic Service environments, focused on operational usability, traceability, and architectural resilience.

We transform meteorological, operational, and normative data into a unified, accessible, and auditable experience to support real-time decision-making.

Design principles

Safety first

Every product evolution starts with operational impact and preserving ATS environment reliability.

Non-invasive architecture

The suite operates as an intelligence layer, preserving the primary system and avoiding unnecessary changes.

Full auditing

Interactions, events, and informational context can be preserved for investigation, analysis, and continuous improvement.

Operational usability

Interfaces and flows are designed for real work contexts, focused on clarity, speed, and low distraction.

Our Values

Principles that guide SOPHIA suite development and our relationship with critical operational environments.

Maximum safety

Commitment to reliable, auditable solutions aligned with ATS environment requirements.

Applied innovation

Development guided by real operational problems, not by disconnected trends.

Operational experience

Solutions built from real operational scenarios and dialogue with people who live the ATS environment.

Technical excellence

Robust architecture, systems thinking, and engineering focused on availability and informational clarity.

Our Journey

An evolution driven by operational maturity, technological integration, and modular expansion.

2025

Proposal foundation

The SOPHIA suite began development with a focus on situational awareness, operational safety, and integrating data relevant to the controller.

2026

First structured module

SOPHIA APP became the main front, combining meteorological data, alerts, and support for approach operations.

2026

Suite expansion

Conceptual evolution of the TWR, ACC, FIS, and AFIS modules, preparing the platform for a broader multimodular architecture.

through 2027

Institutional consolidation
